IGS

After finishing my studies, I was offered a position at Geophysic and Systems Engineering (IGS), a company that provides technological solutions for monitoring weather, renewable energy, and water quality, focusing on hydrology and meteorology. I joined the team for a project to renew the meteorological network for Mexico's National Meteorological Service.

As part of this role, I traveled across various urban areas in Mexico to conduct inspections, installations, maintenance, and troubleshooting of meteorological stations. My responsibilities also included ensuring the proper acquisition and transmission of meteorological data from the network.
